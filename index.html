Welcome

Hello, I'm Davi SAS.

I have always been fascinated by the graphical world. I started exploring design and digital art, diving deep into 3D modeling software like ZBrush, Maya, Blender, and Substance Painter. My passion for visual creation led me to develop solid skills in Hard Surface (Box Modeling), Organic Modeling (Sculpting), Retopology, UV Mapping, Rigging, and Texturing.

In recent years, my curiosity and passion for software development began to grow. I gradually became immersed in the development field, especially JavaScript. The flexibility and power of this language allowed me to transform ideas into functional realities, from simple web pages to complex applications.

Working as a freelancer, I now collaborate with various developers and agencies, applying my knowledge in both front-end and back-end development. My experience spans HTML, CSS, Vanilla JavaScript, React, Angular, Node.js, and MongoDB, enabling me to contribute significantly to a wide range of projects.
